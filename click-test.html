What is CPS?

CPS, also an acronym for "Clicks Per Second", is a stat of a sort that shows how fast you can click. This can be helpful when playing a first person shooter game, such as Minecraft, especially during a fight, when timing is of upmost importance.

Why do I need a better CPS?

As I said before, in Minecraft PvP, timing is pretty important. This is important is because it's almost always about who attacks first. When you have a faster CPS than your opponent, you are able to hit sooner than your opponent when they enter your range, not only giving you the first hit, but the tactical advantage as well.
